Egg Salat

Description

Cut one riped avocado. Add some salat of your choice. Add olives and cherry tomatoes. Cook eggs in multi cooker, cut them and add to the dish. Add olive oil and some vinegar. Add hemp seeds. Mix everuthing and enjoy the tasty and healthy meal full good fats and protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 Big Avocado
  • 1 cup of salat
  • 5-7 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 full eggs
  • 10 egg whites
  • 1 Tbsp of Olive oil
  • 10 Olives
  • 1 Tbsp of Hemp seeds

Steps

  1. Load 12 eggs into multi cooker
  2. Cut avocado in two halves and squeeze each half to the bowl
  3. Add salat, tomatoes and olives
  4. Peel off and extract egg whites
  5. Wash eggs and add to the bowl
  6. Add olive oil and hemp seeds
  7. Mix everything and enjoy
