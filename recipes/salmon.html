Salmon punch

Description

Protein packed meal with some steamed vegetables, which include broccoli, asparagus and brussel sprouts.

Ingredients

  • 125g of salmon
  • 100g of brussel sprouts
  • 5 strands asparagus
  • 150g of broccoli
  • Olive oil
  • Flaxseeds
  • Lemon juice
  • Basil & thyme spices

Steps

  1. Peel off salmon's skin and cut to pieces
  2. Wash vegetables and cut brocoli
  3. Add salmon and vegetables to multi-cooker
  4. Add spices
  5. After cooking add olive oil, flaxseeds and lemon juice
