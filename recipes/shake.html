Protein shake

Description

Anti-oxidant packed, heavenly light and sweet shake that makes your muscles grow and recover with maximum efficiency

Ingredients

  • 50g of Wild blueberries
  • 5g of Creatine
  • 10g of Magnesium
  • 5g of Spirulina
  • 1 scoop of plant Protein powder
  • 10g of Psyllium husk
  • 350ml of Almond milk

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to the mixer
  2. Blend it
  3. Drink it
